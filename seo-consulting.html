Why Most SEO Fails (And How We Fix It)

Most businesses treat SEO as a checklist: insert keywords, publish a blog post, and pray. That strategy stopped working five years ago. Today's search engines—and modern AI Answer Engines—care about semantic relevance, entity authority, and technical perfection. As an SEO consultant based in Ahmedabad working with global clients, my job is to build a moat around your digital presence.

Technical SEO: The Foundation

What does a technical SEO consultant do? A technical SEO consultant analyzes the underlying architecture of a website—like raw crawlability, Core Web Vitals, JSON-LD schema, and indexing blocks—to ensure search engines can read and index pages efficiently without rendering issues.

If Google can't effectively crawl your JavaScript or understand your page hierarchy, the best content in the world won't rank. I dive deep into server logs, fix cumulative layout shifts (CLS), and implement advanced schema markup so bots know exactly what your business limits are.

AEO & GEO: The Next Evolution of Search

How is AEO different from traditional SEO? While traditional SEO focuses on retrieving blue links for search queries, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) structures content to directly answer user questions, primarily targeting 'Position Zero' featured snippets and AI overviews like ChatGPT responses.

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)? Generative Engine Optimization focuses on ensuring your brand and data are accurately integrated into the knowledge graphs and training models used by generative AI like Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini.

I optimize your content so that when a potential customer asks an AI tool for the "best [your service] near me", your business is the one consistently cited.

Local SEO & GMB Domination

How long does local SEO take to work? Significant improvements in local 'Map Pack' visibility often take between 60 to 90 days. This timeline depends on keyword competition, baseline authority, and citation velocity.

For local businesses (clinics, real estate, restaurants), the Google My Business (GMB) profile is the most crucial asset. I treat your GMB listing like a secondary homepage, constantly updating posts, managing review velocity, and optimizing category structures to capture hyper-local 'Near Me' queries.
