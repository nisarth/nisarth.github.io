No guesswork

How I Work

Every project follows the same six steps. Not because it's rigid — but because the structure means nothing falls through the cracks and you always know where things stand.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a conversation. No forms, no questionnaires — just a straightforward call where I ask the questions that matter: what you're trying to achieve, what's not working right now, who your customers are, and what success would actually look like for your business. This shapes everything that comes after.

Timeframe: 30 to 60 minutes
02

Audit and Research

Before I recommend anything, I look at what you already have. Your site, your SEO data, your current rankings, your competitors' positioning, and your automation setup (if any). Most businesses have more working in their favour than they realise — and a few things holding them back that are surprisingly easy to fix.

Timeframe: 3 to 7 days depending on scope
03

Strategy

Once I understand where things stand, I build a clear plan. Not a generic one — a strategy made for your specific business, your budget, and your goals. You'll know exactly what we're going to do, in what order, and why. If something doesn't make sense, ask — I'll explain it until it does.

Timeframe: 2 to 5 days
04

Execution

This is where the actual work happens. Clean, precise, on schedule. SEO work gets done — technical fixes, content, link building. Automations get built and tested. Pages get created. I give you regular updates so you always know what's been done and what's coming next. No radio silence.

Timeframe: Ongoing — typically 4 to 12 weeks for initial phase
05

Monitor and Refine

After the initial work is live, I watch the data carefully. Rankings shift. Automation triggers need tuning. Ad campaigns need adjusting. This is when I'm most useful — catching things early and making adjustments before they become problems. Most of the best wins happen in this phase.

Timeframe: Continuous — 30 to 90 days post-launch
06

Report and Scale

You get transparent, readable reports — not spreadsheets dumped in your inbox. You'll see what moved, what didn't, and what we should focus on next. If results are strong, we look at where to go bigger. If something underperformed, we find out why and fix it. Growth is a loop, not a finish line.

Timeframe: Monthly reports, ongoing strategy
