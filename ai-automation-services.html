n8n Workflow Mastery

What is n8n workflow automation? n8n workflow automation is an open-source, extensible platform that connects different APIs and internet services together. It enables businesses to automate repetitive tasks, route leads automatically, and sync CRM data without manual data entry.

Unlike Zapier, which gets extremely costly at scale, I specialize in deploying custom n8n frameworks. This means you aren't penalized for high-volume tasks. If you need 50,000 leads processed and enriched via AI per month, an n8n pipeline handles it seamlessly at a fraction of the cost.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Chatbots

How does an AI Chatbot improve lead generation? An AI chatbot improves lead generation by engaging website visitors instantly 24/7, answering complex product questions via specific knowledge base retrieval, qualifying intent, and routing high-value prospects directly to a sales CRM.

I don't just plug in a basic ChatGPT wrapper. I build RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems. This means the AI only answers questions using your specific company data—your PDFs, your pricing sheets, your past support tickets. It refuses to hallucinate, and it gracefully hands off complex queries to human staff via Slack or WhatsApp.

Where Do We Start?

What processes should a business automate first? A business should first automate their highest-volume, lowest-variance tasks. This generally includes CRM lead entry from webforms, new client onboarding email sequences, basic customer support FAQs, and automated invoice routing.
