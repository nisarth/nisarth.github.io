Nisarth Patel

I started doing SEO out of curiosity — just trying to understand why some websites show up and others don't. That curiosity turned into a career, and the career turned into something I genuinely care about. Four years later, I work with businesses across India and internationally, helping them get found online and convert that traffic into real revenue.

In the last two years, AI automation has become a big part of what I do. Watching a business cut hours of manual work with an n8n workflow — that's just satisfying to see. Same energy as watching a site climb to page one. It's about making the whole system work better.

I'm based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. I've worked with restaurant chains, e-commerce brands, healthcare clinics, real estate agencies, SaaS startups, and local businesses of all shapes. The projects are different. The approach is always the same — understand the problem, build the right solution, and track what actually works.

Background

How I got here

2021

Started freelancing in SEO

Began working with local Ahmedabad businesses, mostly helping them rank better on Google Maps and local search. First clients were a restaurant and a dental clinic — both got solid results within 90 days.

2022

Expanded to web development and digital marketing

Realised that SEO alone only solves part of the problem. Started building performance-first websites and running Google Ads campaigns alongside search work. The two together produced much better results for clients.

2023

Went deeper on technical SEO and AEO

Started working with larger clients and more technically complex websites. Core Web Vitals, schema markup, and answer engine optimisation became a primary focus as AI-driven search began changing how people find information.

2024

Added AI automation as a core service

Started building n8n workflows and AI-powered pipelines for clients. The first automation I deployed saved a real estate agency 15 hours a week. After that, it became a significant part of the business.

2025

Now — full-service digital growth

Working with businesses across India and internationally. The focus is the same as always: clear strategy, precise execution, and results that are easy to measure.
